Becoming a Member

Applying for Membership
  1. A new user account can be requested, by going to ‘Apply to Join’.
  2. When completed, simply click on ‘Request Account’.
  3. Once your request is accepted by the Hosts, you will receive a confirmation email to your email address asking you to create a password for your account. Follow the instructions given in the email. After submitting your user name and your password, you will become a Member.
Logging In
  1. Once you become a Member, you can ‘Log In’ each time you enter the site, in order to use the site, e.g. add resources, join groups, etc.
  2. You can Log In either by using the Log In box in the Welcome page as you enter the site.
  3. Or by clicking on the ‘Log In’ link at the top right corner of the navigation bar which will take you to the Log In form shown here.

Editing Your Profile.

  1. Click on ‘Profile’ at the top right corner of the navigation bar.
  2. Fill in your details.
  3. A pencil icon will appear when you take the curser over the fields, e.g. phone number. Click on the icon and a text box will appear for you to fill in the information, as shown. Then click the ‘Save’ button.
  4. Click on the pencil mark that appears on the image on the left side of the Profile page if you would like to add your photo.
    1. Browse and upload your picture
    2. Click ‘Upload Picture’

Working with Resources

Types of Resources

You can upload wiki pages and documents such as word files, PDF files and power point presentations. Videos and audio clips cannot be uploaded directly but their links can be added to wiki pages. Though you can view and download these resources as well, there will be restrictions depending on the permission settings applied to the resources. For instance, some of the resources will be accessible only to the members of a particular group.

Adding Resources

  1. Go to the group/region to which you want to upload a resource.
  2. Click on the 'Add...' button which can be found on the right side of the group page.
  3. A pop up screen will appear on your screen
  4. Choose if it is going to be an upload of a page or a resource.
  5. Then click 'Create Resource' to add more details about the content before saving it.
  6. Titles of resources should not have more than 60 characters in order to be uploaded. You can shorten them when filling the title box and write the full title as the first line of the description for the resource, as shown in the example below:
    1. E.g. 'MHPSS in Humanitarian Emergencies-What Should General Health Coordinators Know?'
    2. Title: 'What Should General Health Coordinators Know?'
    3. Description: 'MHPSS in Humanitarian Emergencies-What Should General Health Coordinators Know?' This is a useful guide for ...

Joining Regions

[Regions] will help you to connect to relevant Resources and People around the world that are living or working in the that Region. Each region has a Host that will connect you and keep you up-to-date with the latest happenings in your area.

  1. Visit the region you would like to be part of through the 'Regions' Tab in the main navigation.
  2. Select a region that you would like to be part of, eg. Europe
  3. On a Region page you will see a 'Join Region' button on the right hand side. Click this.
  4. You will now start to receive updates telling you when new resources have been added to this region. Your Host will also keep you up-to-date with the latest movements in the network.

Change your permissions settings

You can choose who has permission to do what in your group or on your resource by adjusting the sliders.

  1. To change you permissions setting on any content you have created go to the top right hand corner and look for the Lock symbol.
  2. Click on this lock and a screen will 'pop-up' with sliders.
  3. On the left hand side there is a vertical list of types of users .A creator is the person who made the content. A Host is the administrator for the group.
  4. Along the top there is a list of actions you can give them permission to do. Adjust each slider as you require.
  5. You may also want to adjust the permissions for different areas eg. Group Resources.
  6. Close the box, using the 'x' in the top right hand corner and your permissions will become active.

Using Tags

  • You can add Tags to groups or Resources you find helpful so they are easy to find again.
  • Other members will also be adding tags to content they add.
  • Using the explore page you can filter content using tags by clicking on a Tag to see all the items tagged with that particular tag.
  • You can then filter your search to show results showing any particular tag.

Back to topics

Searching the site

  • A search box is present on every page, simply type in the keywords you are looking for.
  • You can either visit a specific section and refine your search by keeping the refinement box selected or deselect it and do a full search of all the content on the sit.
  • You will not be able to search for group or resource titles longer than 60 characters as they can not be created on the site.
