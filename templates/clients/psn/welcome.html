The Mental Health & Psychosocial Support (MHPSS) Network serves practitioners, organisations, academics, service users, policy-makers and other stakeholders concerned with mental health and psychosocial support in emergencies and situations of adversity.

The MHPSS Network aspires to improve the well-being and mental health of affected communities and persons through

  • Enabling dialogue and collaboration amongst people and diverse communities of practice
  • Sharing state-of-the-art practice and resources
  • Facilitating peer support

Most resources on this site are publicly viewable online. As a member you can also contribute to the online library, join and participate in thematic groups and interact with other members. You will also be able to decide who can view and edit the material that you post on the site.
